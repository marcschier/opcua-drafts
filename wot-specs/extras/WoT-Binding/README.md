# WoT Binding — extras (informative)

Material that supports the [OPC UA — WoT Binding](../../WoT-Binding/OPC-UA-WoT-Binding.md) draft but is **not** part of the normative specification. Nothing here is normative; the specification is self-contained.

## Contents

- [`adr-to-uav-crosswalk.md`](adr-to-uav-crosswalk.md) — a design-input crosswalk mapping the neutral concepts of the two architecture decision records (ADR 0029, ADR 0032) that informed the model and platform vocabulary onto the `uav` terms. It was moved out of the specification body because the normative meaning of every term now lives in Section 6 (concept, usage, examples) and Section 7 (validation rules); the crosswalk is retained here only as informative design provenance.
